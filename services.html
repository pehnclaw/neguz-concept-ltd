Architectural Pillars

Bespoke Fit-Out Engineering.

Master-grade interior execution engineered for high-net-worth duplexes, executive suites, and corporate developments in Abuja.

Pillar 01

Advanced POP Ceiling & Screeding

Multi-tiered recessed POP ceilings engineered with indirect LED trough lighting tracks, perimeter thermal coving, and mirror-smooth acrylic screeding.

Thermal Cavity Insulation: 150mm air-gap buffer reducing solar heat transfer in penthouse suites.
3000K Ambient LED Lighting: Concealed dimmable LED tracks eliminating ceiling glare.
Mirror-Smooth Wall Screeding: 3-coat acrylic plaster preparation ensuring flawless paint adhesion.

Pillar 02

Bespoke Joinery & Floating TV Consoles

Custom-milled fluted charcoal and warm gold wall paneling integrated with floating sintered marble media cabinetry and concealed cable conduits.

Concealed Wire Conduits: Internal wall channelling eliminating all visible power cords and HDMI lines.
Sintered Marble Slabs: Heat and scratch-resistant natural stone finishes for heavy media displays.
Precision Millwork: Custom-slatted timber wall tracking built to exact wall dimensions.

Pillar 03

Acoustic Cinema & Spatial Fit-Out

Turnkey private cinema transformations equipped with acoustic isolation treatment, fiber-optic star ceilings, dark velvet tracking, and custom seating risers.

Acoustic Isolation Panels: High-density soundproofing preventing audio bleed into bedroom suites.
Star-Ceiling Fiber Optics: Micro-illuminated constellation lighting embedded in dark ceilings.
Tiered Seating Risers: Custom structural platforms engineered for multi-row cinema seating.
