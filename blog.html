Best POP Ceiling Designs in Abuja: Thermal Insulation & Ambient Lighting Tiers

Discover how multi-tiered recessed LED troughs transform thermal regulation and visual luxury in high-humidity duplexes across Maitama and Asokoro.

Plaster of Paris (POP) ceiling design in Abuja has evolved far beyond basic perimeter molding. In Nigeria's Federal Capital Territory, where architectural temperatures require clever climate mitigation alongside sleek visual aesthetics, multi-tiered POP engineering plays a vital dual role.

Key Engineering Considerations for Abuja Duplexes:
